History

Rapids! started floating the river in 1995. We have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30 years. We are a family owned and operated business dedicated to giving you the best rafting adventures possible.

Our team has spent years exploring rivers and guiding people through exciting adventures. What began as a small group of passionate guides has grown into a trusted rafting company that helps families and friends create unforgettable memories on the water.
